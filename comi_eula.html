Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 5, 2026 · Contact: Support@comi.uno

These guidelines apply to all dubbing recordings, clip interactions, vote battles, comments, profiles, messages, reports, and community activity on Comi.

Comi is built for fun, creative, respectful anime dubbing entertainment.

1. Community Purpose

Comi is intended to be a positive entertainment community where users can enjoy short dubbing clips, record creative voice performances, vote in battles, discover trending clips, and interact with other users.

Users must follow these Community Guidelines in addition to Comi’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Respectful Participation

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, threats, hateful conduct, stalking, malicious false reporting, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited.

Users may not attack or degrade people based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, medical condition, age, immigration status, veteran status, or similar protected characteristics.

3. Dubbing Content Rules

Users may submit voice recordings, creative interpretations, parody-style performances, entertainment captions, and community comments. Content should be fun, lawful, respectful, and appropriate for the platform.

Users may not submit dubbing content that is illegal, hateful, sexually explicit, exploitative, threatening, defamatory, misleading, infringing, or intended to harass others.

4. Anime Clip and Copyright Rules

Comi respects copyright and intellectual property rights. Short anime-related clips used in the Service are intended to come from publicly available promotional materials, trailers, teasers, previews, public/free resources, or user-authorized sources.

Users must not upload full episodes, full movies, unauthorized copyrighted works, pirated content, copyrighted music without permission, or third-party materials they do not have rights to use.

Rights holders may contact Support@comi.uno regarding copyright concerns. Valid notices may result in removal or restriction.

5. Vote Battles and Fair Play

Vote battles and ranking features are for entertainment and community engagement. Users may not manipulate votes, create fake accounts, use bots, coordinate abuse, buy fake engagement, or misrepresent content ownership.

Comi may remove entries, invalidate votes, adjust rankings, restrict accounts, or suspend users where manipulation or abuse is detected.

6. Prohibited Content

  • Harassment, hate speech, threats, bullying, or intimidation.
  • Sexually explicit, exploitative, or non-consensual content.
  • Graphic violence, gore, terrorism, extremism, or harmful instructions.
  • Scams, phishing, impersonation, fake identities, or deceptive promotions.
  • Copyright infringement or unauthorized distribution of protected works.
  • Spam, bot activity, vote manipulation, or platform abuse.
  • Sharing private information without permission.

7. Privacy and Voice Safety

Do not upload another person’s voice, image, private message, or personal information without permission. Do not use dubbing features to impersonate, defame, harass, or deceive others.

Users are responsible for ensuring they have rights and consent for any voice, text, image, music, or other material they submit.

8. Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with the platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, abuse refunds, manipulate delivery of digital items, or use purchased items to harass or manipulate others.

9. Reporting and Moderation

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, copyright concerns, harassment, scams, impersonation, vote manipulation, explicit material, or other misuse.

Comi may review reports using manual review, automated tools, safety signals, purchase records, moderation history, and community reports.

10. Enforcement

Comi may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, vote invalidation, battle disqualification, feature restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

Severity, repetition, harm level, copyright concerns, legal obligations, and App Store compliance requirements may affect enforcement decisions.

11. Contact

Questions about these guidelines may be sent to Support@comi.uno.
